Search Module


Search the database for a list of relevant radiology records and to view medical images with the zoom-in facility. Similar to "Report Generating Module",


1. enter diagnosis, starting date and finishing date, chooses one of four ways to sort the records, then click "Search"


2. records will be displayed. Records displayed contain all the information, including the list of thumbnails of medical images, of the record.


Viewing pictures


3. Click on a thumnail


4. Redirected to regular size image


5. Click regular size image


6. Redirected to full size image
