Patient User Manual


After logged in as a patient, there are three options in Navigation page:


1. Personal Info Module


Change login infomation: you can change your user name and password.


Change personal infomation: you can change your first name, last name, address, Email, phone number.


2. Search Module


Search the database for a list of relevant radiology records and to view medical images with the zoom-in facility. You can only view your own records. Records displayed contain all the information, including the list of thumbnails of medical images, of the record. You can also choose one of four ways to sort the records.


3. Logout


Log out the current account and return to login page.
